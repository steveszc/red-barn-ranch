What Makes Our Eggs Special

🌾 Truly Organic

Our chickens enjoy certified organic, non-soy, non-GMO feed grown right here in Nebraska. We believe in nurturing God's creatures with the purest nutrition possible.

🐓 Free-Range Living

Our hens roam freely across our 20-acre pasture in a rotational grazing system. We move our home-built mobile chicken coop regularly, ensuring fresh grass and natural foraging while maintaining healthy pasture - the way God intended.

🥚 Farm-Fresh Quality

Eggs are clean but unwashed, preserving their natural protective coating so they stay fresh on your counter for weeks. We honor the natural design of these perfect packages.

📦 Weekly Egg Shares

We offer weekly egg shares - approximately 1 dozen eggs per week. Each share ensures you receive the freshest eggs while supporting sustainable, faith-based farming.

Meet the Szczecina Family

Hi! We're Steve and Laura Szczecina, along with our six kids: Adam, Ben, Josh, Jonah, Isaiah, and Daniel. We've called Northern Colorado home for over 20 years and love sharing our passion for sustainable farming with our community.

Adam, Ben, and Josh are our main chicken caretakers - they handle the daily care of our hens and gather every egg by hand with pride. Our livestock guardian dogs, Rosie and Liberty, keep our flock safe from hawks, coyotes, and other predators.

"We believe our family farm is part of fulfilling God's calling to be fruitful, multiply, and take dominion over the earth. Every day we work to be faithful stewards of the land, animals, and community He's entrusted to our care. It's not just about farming - it's about stewarding His creation with love, integrity, and purpose."

We believe in doing things the right way, even if it's not the easy way. Every egg represents our commitment to quality, sustainability, and treating our animals with the respect they deserve as part of God's creation.

Frequently Asked Questions

How much do egg shares cost?
Our eggs are $7 per dozen. This price reflects the high quality and purity of our eggs, covers our organic feed costs, and provides fair compensation for our boys' hard work in caring for the hens and preparing your eggs.
How do I pay for my egg share?
We run our egg shares like a monthly subscription. We accept payment by check, cash, or Venmo, and ask that you pay monthly for your convenience and ours.
What if I go on vacation or don't need eggs one week?
Our hens keep laying eggs even when you're away! We're happy to hold your eggs for a week if you're out of town. For longer trips, we'd love it if you could find a friend or neighbor to enjoy your share - it's a great way to introduce others to farm-fresh eggs!
How long do unwashed eggs stay fresh?
Unwashed eggs with their natural protective coating can stay fresh on your counter for 2-3 weeks, or in the refrigerator for up to 2 months. The natural bloom keeps them fresher longer than store-bought eggs.
What breeds of chickens do you raise?
Our diverse flock includes ISA Browns, Amberlinks, Rustic Rocks, Midnight Marans, Prairie Bluebells, Cream Legbars, Buff Orpingtons, Rhode Island Reds, Ameraucanas, and Lavender Wyandottes. We have three roosters: Eggman and Doof (Ameraucanas) and Bart (Black Copper Marans). We even have one egg-laying turkey whose large speckled eggs may occasionally appear in your weekly share if you're lucky!
Can we visit the farm?
We love sharing our ranch with egg share members! Please reach out to arrange a visit - we'd be happy to show you around, introduce you to our flock, and share more about our farming practices and faith journey.
What are the exact pickup times and locations?
Specific pickup times and locations will be confirmed when you join our program. We'll provide detailed information for Fort Collins Thursday pickups, Windsor Sunday morning pickups, and on-farm visits.
How far in advance do I need to commit to a share?
We're flexible! We can discuss arrangements that work for your family, whether you want to try it for a month or commit to a full season. We believe in building lasting relationships with our customers and community.
